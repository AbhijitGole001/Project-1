About photography Films

create anywhere

Photography Films was established in 2018 by Shardul Ade to share stories about creatives that “do what they want, not what’s expected” and to prove that you can have a career in the creative world and make a living from it.

Too many times have we heard the term “get a real job” or “come back to the real world” so PF was created as an Photography_Films, an “Photography” real world where anyone can express themselves without judgement.

Now days, more than ever before, people can “create anywhere”.
